Astral Bridge Documentation

Welcome to the official documentation for Astral Bridge, the seamless cross-chain bridge for transferring assets between blockchains with unparalleled speed, security, and low fees.

What is Astral Bridge?

Astral Bridge is a decentralized protocol that enables the seamless transfer of digital assets between different blockchain networks. Our bridge uses advanced cryptographic techniques to ensure secure and fast cross-chain transactions while maintaining the lowest possible fees.

With Astral Bridge, you can easily move your assets across multiple chains including Ethereum, Solana, Binance Smart Chain, Polygon, Arbitrum, Optimism, Base, and Avalanche.

How It Works

Astral Bridge uses a lock-and-mint mechanism to facilitate cross-chain transfers:

1

Lock

Your assets are locked in a secure smart contract on the source blockchain.

2

Verify

Our decentralized validators confirm the transaction on the source blockchain.

3

Mint

Equivalent assets are minted on the destination blockchain and sent to your wallet.

4

Finalize

The transaction is confirmed and your assets are available on the destination chain.

Astral Bridge utilizes a network of decentralized validators to ensure security and prevent single points of failure. The bridge is non-custodial, meaning you always maintain control of your assets throughout the bridging process.

Bridging Assets

Follow these steps to bridge your assets from one blockchain to another:

1

Connect Your Wallet

Click the "Connect Wallet" button in the top-right corner and select your wallet provider.

2

Select Source and Destination Networks

Choose the network you're transferring from and the network you want to transfer to.

3

Select Asset and Amount

Choose the asset you want to bridge and enter the amount.

4

Enter Recipient Address

Enter the wallet address on the destination chain where you want to receive the assets.

5

Confirm and Bridge

Review the transaction details, approve the transaction in your wallet, and wait for the bridging process to complete.
